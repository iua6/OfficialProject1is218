Pytest testing: Step by Step

  • Step 1: Open your project in Pycharm
  • Step 2: Click on the dropdown menu dialog next to the Play button on the top right of Pycharm
  • Step 3: Click your Project name ex:"project1flask"
  • Step 4: Click the play button to run container
  • Step 5: Navigate to services on the bottom middle of Pycharm and you should see your container running
  • Step 6: On the left Navigate to locate your Project folder
  • Step 7: Right Click on your project folder and select create terminal
  • Step 8: On the right run in the services terminal 'pip install pytest-cov
  • Step 9:Then run 'pip install pytest-pylint
  • Step 10:Then run'pip install pytest --pylint --cov
  • Step 11: Whatever failed tests you get, fix them.
